Welcome to Artificer. Most of the game takes place within the keep itself, though there are many places within to explore and secrets to uncover. As the game progresses you will build a small army of minions to conquer this long cursed land. Every day you will need to plan how you will spend your time and what your minions should be doing with theirs. In the morning though you're free to wander the keep, interact with your minions, and do whatever else power mad tyrants tend to do all day.
